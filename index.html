Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ProxiMeet.

ProxiMeet is a real-time, location-based app that helps you discover and connect with people nearby who share your interests or immediate needs — safely and intentionally in public spaces.

Absolutely. Safety is built into every layer. All meetups happen in public spaces, your precise location is never shared, and every profile is verified. You always control who sees you and when.

ProxiMeet uses real-time proximity and shared intent signals to match you with nearby people. Set your interest or need, and the app surfaces compatible people around you right now — no endless swiping.

We're planning a beta launch in Q2 2026, starting in select pilot cities. Join the waitlist to get early access and help shape the product.

ProxiMeet will be free to download and use. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but core matching and safety features will always remain free.

Unlike traditional social apps, ProxiMeet focuses on real-time, in-person connection with built-in safety. No profiles to browse endlessly — just genuine, context-first introductions with people who are actually nearby.
